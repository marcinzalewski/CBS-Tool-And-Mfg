Design

CBS Tool and Manufacturing provides design and engineering services, working closely with clients through all phases of a project, from conceptual schematics, design reviews and iterations, to final design approval, to ensure client requirements and expectations are met.

Manufacturing

Our production capabilities include die building, prototyping, tool-making, and CNC machining. We combine advanced technology with decades of highly-skilled, hands-on experience to create products that are durable, reliable, and built to your exact specifications. We use only American-made materials and manufacture all our products in our facility in Schaumburg, Illinois.
